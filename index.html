The camp

An old-school Dahab camp, run properly.

“Rayga” is Dahab slang for that state of total, salty, sun-warmed calm. We built a camp around it: palm-wood huts and simple rooms wrapped around a green courtyard on Peace Road, steps from the Masbat promenade. No lobby music. No dress code. Just the reef, the mountains across the gulf, and tea that never runs out.
